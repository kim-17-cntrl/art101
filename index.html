About Me

I'm an second year AGPM major and am working towards a FDM minor. I enjoy doing art in my free time. I like using all kinds of mediums from fabric, yarn, felt, oil pastel, pencil, etc. I've been really interested in learning how to throw pottery on a wheel, printing methods like linocut or screenprinting, and how to make stained glass. I also enjoy playing video games, my current favorites right now are Katamari and Splatoon. I'm a big fan of physical media in general. From CD's, cassettes, manga, or zines, I like them all. Hit me up if you ever want to talk about any of these things!
